About the Business

Salon Daba is a loc boutique located in the heart of Hollywood. Originally based in Dakar, Senegal, our founder Amina was taught by her mother and has sought to provide the same skill and quality of West Africa to the local community of Los Angeles. Daba is committed to maintaining the craftsmanship of natural hair as well as generating funds to donate to Seneca. Daba Los Angeles assists in providing a "green" education to underserved children centered around environmentalism.
